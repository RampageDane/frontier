About Us

Learning from past masters and understanding the way of making Kettle Corn, Jack White went on to creating a small business in Southern California in 1996 that would produce thousands of bags of Kettle Corn in the coming years. Known throughout the Farmers markets, others have seen how Frontier has built up their business to many Farmers Markets and events across SoCal, but still resides to be the little Family based Kettle Corn booth that we are known for.
